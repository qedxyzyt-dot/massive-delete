Security & Privacy

Your data safety is our top priority. Here's how we keep it protected.

🔒

Google OAuth 2.0

Authentication is handled entirely by Google. We never see or store your password.

🚫

No Email Storage

We do not read, store, or copy your email content. Emails are processed in real-time for deletion only.

📊

Minimal Logs

We only log timestamps and aggregate metrics (e.g., number of emails deleted). No personal data is retained.

↩️

Revoke Anytime

Remove access at any time via Google Account → Security → Third-party access. One click, done.
