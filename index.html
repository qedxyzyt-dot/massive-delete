How it works

Three steps. No installs.

Everything runs inside Google Apps Script — the same infrastructure that powers Google Sheets macros. Nothing is installed on your computer.

1

Sign in with Google

Standard OAuth 2.0 flow. You're authenticating with Google directly — we never touch your password.

2

Type a sender, hit search

Enter one or more sender names. You'll see a preview of every matching email before anything is deleted.

3

Confirm and delete

Review the list, click delete. Emails are removed permanently — no limbo, no trash folder, just gone.

Why trust this?

We built it for ourselves first.

This tool started as a personal script to clean a Gmail account with 80,000+ promotional emails. The security model is simple: we don't want your data, and we architected it that way.

Google handles authentication

OAuth 2.0 means your password goes to Google, not to us. We receive a scoped token — that's it.

Emails are never stored

We process in real-time to execute your request. When the session ends, nothing is retained. No database, no cache, no copies.

Runs on Google's servers

The code executes inside Google Apps Script's sandbox. Your data stays within Google's infrastructure at all times.

Revoke access anytime

Go to Google Account → Security → Third-party access, and remove us. One click, instant.
